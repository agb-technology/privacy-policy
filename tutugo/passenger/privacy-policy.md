# Privacy Policy

**App:** TuTuGo (Passenger)
**Effective Date:** 2026-06-05
**Developer:** AGB Technology Co., Ltd.
**Contact:** legal@agb-technology.com

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use TuTuGo:

- **Account information** – name, phone number, email address, and profile photo (including information provided when signing in with TikTok).
- **Location data** – real-time GPS location to match you with nearby drivers and show your route.
- **Trip data** – pickup and drop-off addresses, trip duration, and fare.
- **Device information** – device model, OS version, and app version for troubleshooting.
- **Payment information** – transaction records (payment card details are processed by our payment provider and not stored on our servers).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and operate the ride-hailing service.
- Match passengers with available drivers.
- Process payments and send receipts.
- Send trip status notifications and service updates.
- Improve app performance and user experience.
- Comply with legal obligations.

---

## 3. Third-Party Login (TikTok)

When you choose to sign in with TikTok, we receive your TikTok display name and profile picture from TikTok's API. We do not receive your TikTok password or private messages. You can revoke TikTok's access at any time via your TikTok account settings.

---

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

- **Drivers** – your name, pickup location, and trip details to complete your ride.
- **Payment processors** – to handle transactions securely.
- **Service providers** – analytics, crash reporting, and push notification services that operate on our behalf.
- **Authorities** – when required by law or to protect safety.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Trip history is retained for up to 2 years. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at legal@agb-technology.com.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your account and data.
- Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

---

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for data in transit and store sensitive data in encrypted form. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

TuTuGo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
